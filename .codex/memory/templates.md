# Memory Templates

Use these formats when adding entries.

## Setup / Log Entry

`YYYY-MM-DD | AGENT:@codex | FACT`

## Decision Entry

`YYYY-MM-DD | AGENT:@codex | DECISION | WHY | CONSEQUENCES`

## Incident Entry

`YYYY-MM-DD | AGENT:@codex | ISSUE | ROOT CAUSE | STATUS`

## Domain Entry

`YYYY-MM-DD | AGENT:@codex | AREA | DETAIL`
